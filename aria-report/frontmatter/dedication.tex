\begin{minipage}{0.85\textwidth}
\centering

To my mother, \textbf{Faouziya Khmili}, whose patience, prayers, and unconditional love have been my quiet strength through every late night and every doubt.\\[0.6cm]

To my father, \textbf{Ali Mabrouki}, whose hard work, wisdom, and belief in me taught me that nothing worth having comes without effort.\\[0.6cm]
