\begin{center}
{\Large\itshape To those who gave me roots, wings, and the courage to dream.}
\end{center}

\vspace{0.6cm}

\begin{center}
\begin{minipage}{0.9\textwidth}
\centering
\setstretch{1.15}

To my mother, \textbf{Faouziya Khmili}, whose prayers carried me through every doubt and whose love lit the way on the hardest nights.\\
\textit{Your patience is the quiet strength I stand on.}\\[0.4cm]

To my father, \textbf{Ali Mabrouki}, whose quiet sacrifices taught me that nothing worth having comes without effort.\\
\textit{Your belief in me made me believe in myself.}\\[0.4cm]

To my brother, \textbf{Mostapha}, and my sister, \textbf{Rania}, for the laughter that healed stress and the reminder that home is never far, no matter how difficult the road becomes.\\[0.4cm]

This work is yours as much as it is mine. Every line of code, every late night, and every small victory carries a piece of your love.

\vspace{0.6cm}

And to the version of me that refused to quit --- through the bugs, the deadlines, and the uncertainty:\\
\textit{This is for the growth, the grit, and the dream.}

\end{minipage}
\end{center}
